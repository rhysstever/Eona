## Deity Ties
The Prime Divines can be split into two main categories: Ties of Good and Evil, and Ties of Order and Chaos. In addition, each deity has a tie to one or two others. Ties of Good and Evil have three deities each: one good, one neutral, and one evil deity. Ties of Order and Chaos have a lawful deity and a chaotic deity counterpart.
